About Us

We couldn't find the sports car of our dreams.

So we built one.

     We are a team of budding engineers with an aim to successfully build the first SAE SUPRA car of our institution. We are beginning our valuable journey with a set of like-minded, interested students who seek to work on innovative technologies. We look forward to learn from and also face challenges to gain experience which will help us in all our future endeavours.
